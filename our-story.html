OUR STORY

— FROM BANARAS TO DUBLIN —

नमस्ते! I'm Aashna, and I grew up in the ghats of Banaras — where every meal is a celebration, every recipe holds generations of love, and the aroma of baati fills the air.

When I moved to Dublin, I missed that taste of home. So I started cooking the food I grew up eating — the same recipes my baba taught me, made with the same love.

KAASHI is my way of sharing a piece of Banaras with you. Every baati is hand-rolled, every chokha freshly pounded. No shortcuts. Just authentic Banarasi soul food, made in my Dublin kitchen.

🙏 Aapka swagat hai!
